VIETNAM's capital, Hanoi is famous for long history and traditional culture of the nation.

SAPA

Sapa is the destination for you to admire the wild beauty of terraced fields, discover the customs and cultural beauty of the ethnic groups in the mountains.

DANANG

Da Nang is a coastal city with more than 60 km of beach. With beautiful beaches, gentle stretches and endless white sand, Danang Beach was voted by Forbes magazine as one of the 6 most attractive beaches on the planet.

HOCHIMINH CITY

Traveling to Ho Chi Minh City, you come to a city located in the middle of the rich Southern region, bordering the South of the Southeast and the Northern edge of the South West.

PHUQUOC

Phu Quoc, also known as Dao Ngoc, is Vietnam's largest island, and is also the largest of the 22 islands here, located in the Gulf of Thailand.

VIETNAM CULTURE

Vietnam is a country with 54 different ethnic groups, each with its own unique and impressive culture and identity. Having a long tradition over thousands of years, it has become a customary law, deeply and firmly attached to the hearts of every Vietnamese people.

VIETNAM FOOD

Vietnamese cuisine is the name of the principle of mixing spices and the general eating habits of all Vietnamese people in Vietnam. Although there are more or less differences between regions and ethnic groups, Vietnamese cuisine still implies the most general meaning to refer to all popular dishes in the Vietnamese community.
